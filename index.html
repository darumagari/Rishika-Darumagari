Hi, I’m Rishika

With 6+ years of experience, I am a data-driven problem solver, who provides insightful business solutions. I love solving problems using data and technology and learning something new at every step.

After my undergrad in Finance, I explored the world of Business Intelligence and Data Analysis working at Vishal Constructions for a year. I applied data warehouse techniques, visualizations and honed problem-solving skills to analyze and resolve issues.

I graduated with Master’s in Information Systems from San Diego State University where I gained extensive knowledge in the field of Database Management Systems and Statistical Analysis. Along with my Master's, I interned at Phone.com where my key contributions were supporting the Marketing Ops & Sales teams in reporting and analysis.

After Graduation, I worked as a Data Analyst with Aberdeen for two years where I leverage innovative analytical techniques to uncover patterns and trends in data using MSSQL and create interactive dashboards using Tableau for senior leaders to make an informed decision about customers, products, and competitors. I also partnered with our data scientist to develop modeling, structuring and business rules to enable project execution. It was an amazing learning experience.

Currently, I work as a Senior Data Analyst for Ford Motor Company. I was responsible for creating and developing solutions for business problems using data analytics.

In addition to my formal degrees, I have earned several certifications including Python 101, The Numpy Stack in Python, Python for Data Science and Machine Learning Boot Camp etc.
